Data Storage and Databases

Data storage encompasses the methods and technologies used to preserve and retain data for future use. It includes a variety of storage technologies like hard disk drives, solid-state drives, cloud storage, tape storage, and optical storage. These methods provide options for storing data digitally or physically, catering to different needs and preferences.
