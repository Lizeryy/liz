Backend Development

Backend development focuses on building and maintaining the server-side components of websites and applications. It involves creating the logic, databases, and infrastructure that handle data processing, storage, and overall system functionality. Backend developers use various programming languages and frameworks to ensure the smooth operation and performance of digital platforms, integrating with frontend interfaces and external services to deliver seamless user experiences.
