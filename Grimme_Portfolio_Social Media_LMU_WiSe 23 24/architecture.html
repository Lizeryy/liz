Architecture and Infrastructure

In essence, the client-server architecture delineates the interaction between clients and servers,
serving as a fundamental computing model prevalent in computer networks and distributed systems.
In summary, the client-server architecture relies on an intricate infrastructure comprising diverse components
and technologies to facilitate communication between clients and servers.
