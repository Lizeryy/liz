Key Concepts

Online media encompasses all forms of content and information created, distributed, and consumed through the internet or digital channels. This includes websites, blogs, news articles, videos, podcasts, online magazines, social media platforms, and more. These technologies, including 5G networks, IoT, AI, blockchain, cloud computing, AR/VR, quantum computing, and cybersecurity, are revolutionizing various aspects of the digital world and driving innovation across industries.
